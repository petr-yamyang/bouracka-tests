## Env-specific divergences

DEMO is the driving-school variant with **reduced validation** per
C-5:
- DEMO may skip OCR auto-fill (manual entry only).
- DEMO may bypass reCAPTCHA (the wizard is exercised many times per
  day by school instructors — a CAPTCHA wall would block them).
- DEMO may use a stub SMS gateway that always returns OTP `0000` for
  predictability.
- DEMO may produce a "DEMO ONLY — NEINSTRUKČNÍ" watermark on the PDF.

All of the above is **hypothesis** until user-supplied recon
templates land.
